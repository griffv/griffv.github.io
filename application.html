Who is Snowblind?

Snowblind, after taking a break from BFA for Ny'alotha and part of EP IS BACK!

We strive to get everyone a chance to get in on progression, so we try to keep from having a massive roster. We currently are fielding a roster of 25 people. If you still feel that your application can stand out from the pack and our current raiders/trials we will always review exceptional applicants.

Interested? Fill out an application today!

Snowblind Application

Note: Our applications are reviewed by our raiders in addition to our officers. Fields with a * are required.

* What name do you prefer to go by? (First Name, Nickname, Character Name etc.)


* Discord Tag


* BattleTag


* What is your main class/spec?


* Please provide a link to your character's Warcraftlogs page.


* How did you hear about Snowblind?


* Tell us a little bit about yourself.


* Outline your relevant raiding experience, including the guilds you were in previously and why you left them.


If your past raiding experience is on other characters, please share the logs for those characters as well.


If you have any stream vods/recordings showcasing your play, please share.


* Tell us why you think you'd be a great addition to our roster. What are your strengths, what sets you apart etc.


If there's anything not covered in the previous questions you'd like to share, feel free.


What are we looking for?

-Put in the time and effort to excel at your role despite our limited schedule.

-95%+ attendance. We aim to keep a tight roster so everyone gets opportunities to play. We understand that things do come up so everyone has to miss from time to time. But it can’t be a trend.

- Show up on time ready and focused.

- Don’t be afraid to talk.

Raid times:

We raid every Tuesday and Thursday.

Raids start at 8:30PM Eastern and end at 12:30AM Eastern. (5:30PM to 9:30PM pacific)

We want to make the most out of the 8 hours we’ve all committed to. With that said Snowblind is host to a diverse and wide ranging community of good people. Even if you’re able to slam bosses, being an abrasive ass won’t get you far here. We aim to create an environment where everyone can have a good time without wasting other people’s time so keeping comms clear during a pull is a must but we also don’t expect you to just be a robot, nor do we want that.
